### About CodeRIT
CodeRIT is the official coding club of Ramaiah Institute of Technology (RIT), aiming to foster a strong coding culture among students. It supports learners of all levels with resources, events, and mentorship to excel in programming and technology.

---

### Events
CodeRIT organizes various workshops, hackathons, and seminars to enhance coding skills and technical knowledge:
- **Workshops:** Covering topics like competitive programming, data structures, algorithms, Git/GitHub, and technologies like Flask and Python.
- **Hackathons:** Events like "WHackiest" encourage creative problem-solving in coding with mentorship and prizes.
- **Tech Talks:** Sessions focused on resume building, interview preparation, and industry insights.
- **Special Events:** For example, "Ice Breaker 2024" introduces new members to CodeRIT activities and the coding ecosystem.

Visit the [CodeRIT Events Page](https://coderit.netlify.app/events) for more details.

### Recruitment
CodeRIT typically recruits students in their 3rd year of study through a series of rounds determined by the senior core members. The recruitment process includes:
- Resume shortlisting
- Group discussion
- One-on-one interviews

**Can students from any course participate?**
Yes, students from any course can participate in the recruitment process.

 GENERAL RULES

1. **Team Composition**: Each team must consist of exactly 3 members.
2. **Team Registration**: All teams must register before the event starts and will be assigned a unique Team ID.
3. **Identity Verification**: All participants must carry their college/university ID cards throughout the event.
4. **Time Management**: Participants are required to be present at the designated locations on time. Late arrivals may result in disqualification.
5. **Code of Conduct**: Participants must maintain a respectful and professional demeanor throughout the event. Any form of misconduct will lead to immediate disqualification.
6. **Electronic Devices**: Teams are allowed to use only one laptop/device during the event. Use of multiple devices is prohibited.
7. **Internet Access**: Internet access is permitted only for specific documentation and reference purposes. Accessing third-party help or solutions is strictly prohibited.
8. **Plagiarism**: Any form of plagiarism will result in immediate disqualification. All code must be original and written during the event.
9. **Disputes**: Any disputes or clarifications regarding rules will be resolved by the CodeRIT Core Team, whose decision will be final and binding.
10. **Communication**: Teams are not allowed to communicate with other teams during the competition. Any form of collaboration between teams will lead to disqualification.
11. **Scoring System**: Points from all rounds will be accumulated to determine the final rankings.
12. **Disqualification**: CodeRIT reserve the right to disqualify any team found violating any of the rules.
13. **Rule Changes**: CodeRIT reserve the right to modify any rules if deemed necessary. Any changes will be communicated to all participants before the affected round begins.
